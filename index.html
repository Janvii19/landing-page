Green Salads

Lettuce, spinach, and other salad greens are an important part of a healthful diet
because they can be year-round sources of vitamin A, vitamin C, and other nutrients.
Red and dark green leafy vegetables are generally higher in antioxidants,
Vitamin B6, and other nutrients than lighter colored greens.
Some of the most nutritious greens include spinach, kale, romaine, watercress, and arugula.
They are rich in a combination of vitamins A, C, and K; several B
vitamins (including folate); and >potassium. But some greens aren't nutrient powerhouses.
